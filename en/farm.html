Production

Our own farms produce olive oil, rare ingredients, and Japanese tea (matcha). The farm properties are also available for rent as agri-cultural hubs in an exceptional natural setting.

Drawing from our hands-on farming experience, we continuously develop the latest agricultural technologies — including farm solutions powered by soil sensing and AI analysis.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy your products?

Orders are processed via our contact form. An online shop is in preparation. For wholesale enquiries, please get in touch directly.

Do you ship to Japan?

Yes, we handle international shipping from Italy. Costs vary depending on the format and destination.

Can I visit the farm?

Yes, by appointment. See the Farm Rental section for available options.

What is the difference between the two matcha types?

Usucha is ideal for the classic tea ceremony (whisked with a chasen). Koicha Premium is more concentrated, suited to the formal ceremony and advanced culinary use.
