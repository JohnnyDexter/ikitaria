FAQ

Essential answers on how we structure Italy–Japan collaborations.

Who is IKITARIA for?

We work with companies, professional practices, and project teams developing relationships between Italy and Japan.

How does a collaboration begin?

We start with an initial conversation to clarify objectives, context, and priorities, then define a realistic project scope.

Do you act as a direct importer or exporter?

No. IKITARIA operates as a strategic and cultural advisory partner, coordinating with technical specialists when needed.

How long does a project usually last?

It depends on complexity. We can support a single phase or structure an ongoing mid- to long-term path.

Can I request advice on a specific case?

Yes. We assess focused requests with a selective approach, prioritizing projects aligned with our methodology.
