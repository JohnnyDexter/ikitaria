BtoB Support

We receive numerous consultations for cross-country business between Asia and Europe, offering support in market research, sales representation, local accompaniment, and introductions to the right contacts.

We have a proven track record: commissioned by an Italian manufacturer, we developed new Asian markets and reached an agreement with a major trading company, resulting in significant revenue growth. We also run many pop-up events in Tokyo and welcome enquiries about event participation.

What we offer

Market Research

In-depth analysis of the target market — competitors, consumers, distribution channels, industry trends. Structured, actionable reports.

Sales Representation

We act as your local representative in Japan or Italy, managing commercial relationships, negotiations and follow-up with selected partners.

Local Accompaniment

On-site support during trade missions, trade fairs and partner meetings. Cultural and logistical interpretation included.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which countries do you cover?

We specialise in the Italy–Japan corridor, with hands-on presence in both markets. We also consider projects across the broader Europe–Asia landscape.

Do you work with small businesses or start-ups?

Yes, regardless of company size. Every project is evaluated individually based on objectives and feasibility.

We do not guarantee specific outcomes, but operate with full transparency: regular updates, structured reports, and direct access to the team at every stage.
