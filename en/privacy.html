Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Data Controller

Ikitaria — info@ikitaria.com

Data Collected

Through the contact form we collect: name, email address, and message content. This data is transmitted to Formspree Inc. to deliver your message to our team.

No other personal data is collected automatically, and no user profiles are created.

Purpose of Processing

Data is processed solely to respond to user enquiries. It is not used for commercial purposes, profiling, or shared with third parties beyond what is described here.

Third Parties

Formspree Inc.

Processes contact form data. Privacy policy: formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy

Google LLC — Google Fonts

Typefaces are loaded from Google servers (fonts.googleapis.com). Your IP address may be transmitted to Google during loading. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Data Retention

Data is kept for as long as necessary to handle your request, unless longer retention is required by law.

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under Regulation (EU) 2016/679 you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object to, and request portability of your personal data. To exercise these rights contact us at info@ikitaria.com.

Cookies

This site does not use its own profiling or tracking cookies. Google Fonts may set technical third-party cookies during font loading.
