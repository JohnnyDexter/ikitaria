IKITARIA

Ikitaria is an Italy–Japan advisory practice operating as a strategic bridge between the two markets. We build structured professional relationships, turning cultural differences into operational opportunities. Our active presence in both countries ensures continuity, method, and direct market knowledge.

Three Areas

Production

Our own farms produce olive oil, rare ingredients, and Japanese tea (matcha). The farm properties are also available for rent as agri-cultural hubs in an exceptional natural setting.

Brand

As a bridge between Asia and Europe, we organise events, manage communities, and produce our own branded products.

BtoB Support

We receive numerous consultations for cross-country business between Asia and Europe, offering support in market research, sales representation, local accompaniment, and introductions to the right contacts.
